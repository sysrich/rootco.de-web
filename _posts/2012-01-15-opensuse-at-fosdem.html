FOSDEM 2012 is nearly upon us, and once again openSUSE is going to be there in force.

Our stand is going to be on the Ground Floor of Building K from 9am on Saturday 4th February till 6pm on Sunday 5th February

As well as demonstrating openSUSE 12.1, we hope to be showing off the latest successes from the openSUSE ARM Team

We're planning on having DVDs to give away, and T-Shirts, Beer and who knows what else to 'sell' for donations, with all proceeds going back to the FOSDEM organisers as a thank you for hosting this great event.

We've already got a number of volunteers to help with the booth, but we could still do with more - we're expecting it to be really busy.

If you'd like to lend a hand, either drop a message here, or email me at RBrownCCB@opensuse.org , or just turn up on the day and pitch in.

The openSUSE community will not just be there at the booth though, if you take a at the great schedule posted on the FOSDEM site you'll spot several talks, sessions & devrooms that will have openSUSE people taking part.

Hope to see many of you there in a few weeks!
